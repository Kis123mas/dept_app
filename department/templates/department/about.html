About Us

The most gorgeous department to be part of in University Of Calabar.

Welcome to the vibrant and dynamic Computer Science Department at our school, where innovation meets education! Our department is a hub of intellectual curiosity and technological advancement, committed to shaping the future leaders of the digital age.

Unlimited Possibilities

Vision

To be one Nigeria's leading enviroment for learning, teaching and researching in Computer Science, Information Technology and Informatics, collaborating locally and internationally to strenthen the Computer Science profession.

Our Mission

Provide quality education to both undergraduate and graduate students in the areas of theoritical and applied Computer Science to enable them solve real-world problems. Conduct research to advance the Computer Science Body of Knowledge, and apply the new knowledge to relevant areas of human endeavour. Provide education in Computers and their applications to students from other department of the University of Calabar.

Objectives

To give students a broad and deep understanding of the fundamental concepts and principles underlying the technology and workings of computer and computing devices.

To prepare students for career paths in Computer Science through in-programme industrial work experience; To produce relevant and efficient Information Technology personnel to meet the ever-growing needs of both public and private sectors of economies, be it local, state or national.
